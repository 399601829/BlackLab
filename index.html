== What is BlackLab? ==

BlackLab is a corpus retrieval engine built on top of [http://lucene.apache.org/ Apache Lucene]. It allows fast, complex searches with accurate hit highlighting on large, tagged and annotated, bodies of text. It was developed at the Institute of Dutch Lexicology (INL) to provide a fast and feature-rich search interface on our historical and contemporary text corpora.
